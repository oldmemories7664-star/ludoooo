Ludo Rules

Rule 1: Roll to Unlock

You must roll a 6 to move a pawn out of your home area.

Rule 2: Extra Turn

Rolling a 6 gives you an additional turn.

Rule 3: Three Strikes

Three consecutive 6s cancel your turn.

Rule 4: Capture

Land on an opponent's pawn to send it back home.

Rule 5: Safe Zones

Pawns on safe squares cannot be captured.

Rule 6: Exact Dice

You need the exact dice value to enter home.

Rule 7: Win Condition

First player to bring all 4 pawns home wins!
