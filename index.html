How to Play Ludo

Game Objective

Be the first player to move all four pawns from your home to the finish area.

Setup

  • 2-4 players can play
  • Each player has 4 pawns of their color
  • Pawns start in the home area

Basic Rules

  • Rolling the Dice: Players take turns rolling the dice
  • Moving Out: You need to roll a 6 to move a pawn out of home
  • Extra Turn: Rolling a 6 gives you another roll
  • Movement: Move your pawn the number of squares shown on the dice
  • Capturing: Land on opponent's pawn to send them home
  • Safe Zones: Certain squares are safe from capturing
  • Home Stretch: Once all pawns are out, move them into the finish area
  • Winning: First player to get all pawns to finish wins!

Special Rules

  • You can only move if you have at least one pawn out or roll a 6
  • You cannot block with multiple pawns on the same square
  • Maximum 3 consecutive turns (only with extra rolls from 6s)
